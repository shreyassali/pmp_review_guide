How to use this App
Note: This is not a study guide. It is a quick reference review guide.

Before using this app make sure you have completed PMP® Exam Prep by Rita Mulcahy or Head First PMP or any other study book. Before you pick up PMP / CAPM Exam Quick Reference make sure you are familiar with each term from PMBOK, Study guide. Start reading this app around 30-40 days before your exam. Make sure you read it every morning and evening once. If it is not possible to read twice; try to read at least once in a day. This app will allow you to review each critical concepts and refresh your memory. This app also contains free practice test from various sites. Practicing a maximum number of questions will tune your mind to answer questions in short period and increase your stamina to sit continuously for 4 hours. In the end, you will feel confident when you will enter the exam room on the day of your exam. Please reach out at pmpquickreference@gmail.com if you need one on one session on any PMP topic. I wish you all the best for your PMP preparation.
